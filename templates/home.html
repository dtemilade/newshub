ABOUT

Project

This project started because I wanted to make it easy for people to read news from different places in one simple place.

I talked to a friend about how many people now get their news online.
We thought it would be helpful to create a website or app where you can find news from lots of different places easily.

My friend told me about APIs, which are tools that developers use to add features to websites.These APIs can help users stay updated with what's happening in the world.

"NewsHub" is a project that aims to meet the different needs and preferences of its users.

When the opportunity came to build the project as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, it became a challenge because my friend was busy with another project, so I had to work on it alone.

I had about a month to finish the project, with weekly goals to achieve.

It was tough to meet the deadlines and accomplish the plans.
I used resources from various places to build the project.

While there's still room for improvement and updates, I'm thankful that the project was successful.
Now you have NewsHub, designed to give you easy access to news at your fingertips.
